Healing Odyssey

Cultural and Healing Music Project

Check Packages

My business idea is to create a unique cultural and healing music project that uses South Asian traditional music, dance, and storytelling to promote emotional healing, cultural understanding, and spiritual connection. I offer live performances, interactive workshops, and wellness programs tailored for diverse audiences such as elderly care centers, schools, yoga studios, and community organizations in Sweden. By blending multilingual singing, live instruments like tabla and harmonium, and expressive dance, I provide meaningful experiences that bridge cultures and foster joy, peace, and togetherness.

My company primarily targets Sri Lankans, South Asians, and individuals living in Sweden who are connected to or interested in South Asian music, culture, and spiritual healing. These include people seeking emotional connection, cultural identity, and inner peace through music rooted in tradition.

Building from that foundation, we also serve and collaborate with:



1. Cultural and Educational Institutions

• Schools, SFI programs, and universities looking for meaningful multicultural experiences
• Cultural centers and museums interested in showcasing authentic South Asian traditions through performance and workshops

2. Elderly Care Centers

• Elder homes and senior centers, where music can bring comfort, stimulate memory, and foster emotional connection across generations

3. Wellness & Spiritual Communities

• Yoga studios, meditation centers, and spiritual retreats that value healing music, devotional mantras, and emotional well-being through sound

4. Non-Profit Organizations & Community Services

• Red Cross, volunteer services, and integration-focused NGOs seeking cultural and emotional support for refugees, newcomers, and vulnerable communities
• Collaborative programs for emotional healing, cultural exchange, and social inclusion through music

5. Festivals & Public Events

• Multicultural festivals, city events, and community gatherings where music can bridge cultures and inspire connection

6. Private Clients & Online Audience

• Individuals or families looking for personalized healing sessions, music lessons, or cultural experiences
• A global online audience through platforms like YouTube, Instagram, or Spotify for spiritual and emotional music

7. Collaborators & Artists

• Musicians, dancers, spiritual teachers, and storytellers interested in cross-cultural performance and creation
